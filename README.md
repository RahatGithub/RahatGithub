**A general description.** The subject is a full-stack developer with hands-on experience designing and building production-grade SaaS platforms, e-commerce systems, and management applications. Specimens of this variety have been observed moving fluidly across the modern stack — *React, Next.js, Node.js, Django* — and display particular interest in three behaviors: **multi-tenant SaaS architecture**, **real-time systems**, and **scalable backends**.

The specimen is most readily identified by its tendency to write code it would itself want to maintain — a trait surprisingly uncommon in the wild.

## §  III. &nbsp; OBSERVED BEHAVIORS

> *The following has been recorded over multiple sessions of observation.*

❧ &nbsp; **The specimen is most active in the late evening,** displaying patterns consistent with deep-focus work. During these periods it has been observed remaining motionless for hours, save for the rhythmic motion of typing.

❧ &nbsp; **It is frequently observed near caffeine sources** and shows a strong territorial attachment to its development environment.

❧ &nbsp; **Mating call** *(rare)*: a low, satisfied murmur of *"it works on my machine."*

❧ &nbsp; **Defensive posture** *(when challenged on architecture choices)*: a calm, evidence-based explanation, occasionally accompanied by diagrams.

❧ &nbsp; **Migratory pattern**: oscillates seasonally between codebase and documentation.
